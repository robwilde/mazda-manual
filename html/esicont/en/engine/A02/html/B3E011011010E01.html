1. Measure the piston outer diameter using a micrometer.The measurement position is ZJ: 8.7 mm {0.34 in}, ZY and Z6: 8.5 mm {0.33 in} from the lower end of the piston, and in the thrust direction.

ZJ: 73.970-73.980 mm {2.9122-2.9125 in}
ZY, Z6: 77.970-77.980 mm {3.0697-3.0700 in}

2. Calculate the piston-to-cylinder clearance from the cylinder bore inner diameter and the piston outer diameter.

• If it exceeds the maximum specification, replace the piston or the cylinder block.
