5TH GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE PREINSPECTION

A6E511201029M02

1. Remove the rear cover.

2. Measure the 5th gear thrust clearance using a dial indicator.

• If the clearance exceeds the maximum, check the contact surfaces of 5th gear and the clutch hub. Replace worn or damaged parts.
Clearance
0.100-0.220 mm {0.0040-0.0086 in}
Maximum
0.270 mm {0.0106 in}
