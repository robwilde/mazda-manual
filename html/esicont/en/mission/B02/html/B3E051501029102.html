5TH GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE PREINSPECTION

B3E051501029102

1. Remove the rear cover.

2. Measure the 5th gear thrust clearance using a dial indicator.

• If it exceeds the maximum specification, inspect the contact surfaces of 5th gear and the clutch hub. Replace worn or damaged parts.
Standard clearance
0.06-0.26 mm {0.0024-0.0102 in}
Maximum clearance
0.31 mm {0.0122 in}
