AUTO LEVELING CONTROL MODULE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851031W02

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the glove compartment. (SeeGLOVE COMPARTMENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Disconnect the auto leveling control module connector.

4. Remove the screw, then remove the auto leveling control module.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
