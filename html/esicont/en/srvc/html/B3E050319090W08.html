NO.4 EXCESSIVE CREEP

B3E050319090W08

4

Excessive creep

DESCRIPTION

• Vehicle accelerates in D, M ranges and R position when accelerator pedal is not depressed.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

1. Engine idle speed is high (transaxle system is not cause of problem)
2. Go to No.9 "FAST IDLE/RUNS ON" (See NO.9 FAST IDLE/RUNS ON [ZJ, Z6].)
(See NO.9 FAST IDLE/RUNS ON [LF].)
