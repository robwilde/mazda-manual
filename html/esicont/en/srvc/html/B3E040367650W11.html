NO.7 THERE IS MALFUNCTION IN THE SYSTEM EVEN THOUGH ABS WARNING LIGHT, BRAKE SYSTEM WARNING LIGHT, DO NOT ILLUMINATE

B3E040367650W11

7

There is a malfunction in system even though ABS warning light, BRAKE system warning light, do not illuminate.

[TROUBLESHOOTING HINTS]

• There is a difference in size or air pressure between the front and rear tires.

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

INSPECT ABS HYDRAULIC UNIT

Yes
