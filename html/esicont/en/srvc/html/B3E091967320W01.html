WINDSHIELD WIPER ARM AND BLADE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091967320W01

1. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Cap
2
Nut
3
Windshield wiper arm
(See Windshield Wiper Arm Installation Note.)
4
Windshield wiper blade

2. Install in the reverse order of removal.

3. Adjust the windshield wiper arm and blade. (See WINDSHIELD WIPER ARM AND BLADE ADJUSTMENT.)

Windshield Wiper Arm Installation Note

1. Clean the windshield wiper arm connector shafts using a wire brush before installing the windshield wiper arms.
