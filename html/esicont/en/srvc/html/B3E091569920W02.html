FRONT DRAIN HOSE INSTALLATION

B3E091569920W02

Caution
• If the front drain hose is pinched or bent at any point, the water in the hose may not drain and could leak inside the vehicle. During and after installation of the trims and headliner, always make sure there is no interference with the front drain hose. Correct any abnormality if found.

1. Apply soapy water to the part of the sunroof frame where the front drain hose is inserted.

2. Insert the front drain hose end into the sunroof frame.

3. Install the front drain hose to the clips parallel to the pillar and free of looseness.

4. Insert the front drain hose grommet into the hole of the inner hinge pillar.

5. Install the following parts:

(1) Dashboard (See DASHBOARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Front door (See FRONT DOOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Headliner (See HEADLINER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Assist handle (See ASSIST HANDLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Sunvisor (See SUNVISOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6) Interior light (See INTERIOR LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(7) Map light (See MAP LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(8) C-pillar trim (See C-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(9) Trunk side upper trim (5HB) (See TRUNK SIDE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(10) Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(11) Rear seat (See REAR SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(12) B-pillar upper trim (See B-PILLAR UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(13) B-pillar lower trim (See B-PILLAR LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(14) Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(15) Front scuff plate (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(16) Upper anchor of the front seat belt (See FRONT SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(17) A-pillar trim (See A-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(18) Console (4SD) (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
