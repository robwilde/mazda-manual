ELECTRIC POWER STEERING OIL PUMP (LF) CONSTRUCTION

B3E061432650T02

• Electric power steering oil pump has been adopted for vehicles with the LF engine to reduce engine load. This allows for an improvement in fuel economy while conserving the performance and feeling of conventional hydraulic power steering.

• Size and weight reduction is achieved due to the integration of the electric power steering oil pump, motor, reserve tank, and EHPAS control module.
