EXHAUST SYSTEM OUTLINE [ZJ, Z6]

B3E011500115T01

Features

• The exhaust piping is shorter due to the placement of the exhaust manifold at the rear of the engine for improved exhaust performance. Moreover, due to the shorter distance to the catalytic converter, temperature decrease of exhaust emission is prevented and catalyst is more efficient.

• Double wall piping has been adopted for the exhaust manifold to prevent lowering of exhaust gas temperature and facilitates rapid catalyzer activation after cold engine starting for improved exhaust purification.
