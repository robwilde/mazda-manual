FUEL-FILLER LID OPENER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [R.H.D.]

B3E091466640W02

1. To remove the fuel-filler lid opener, remove the following procedure:

• 5HB
- Remove the hole cover on the trunk side trim. (RH)

• 4SD
- Remove the trunk end trim (See TRUNK END TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
- Slightly bend back the trunk side trim. (RH) (See TRUNK SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. To remove the fuel-filler lid opener lever, remove the following part:.

(1) Front scuff plate (driver's side) (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. When removing the fuel-filler lid opener cable, perform the following procedure:

4. Remove the following parts:

(1) Rear scuff plate (driver's side) (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Rear seat (See REAR SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Tire house trim (driver's side) (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) B-pillar lower trim (driver's side) (See B-PILLAR LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Front seat (driver's side) (See FRONT SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5. Partially peel back the floor covering so that the fuel-filler lid opener cable can be removed.

6. Remove the fuel-filler lid opener cable from clips A.

7. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

8. Install in the reverse order of removal.

.

Lift spring
2
