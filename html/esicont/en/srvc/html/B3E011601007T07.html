POSITIVE CRANKCASE VENTILATION (PCV) VALVE FUNCTION [ZJ, Z6, LF]

B3E011601007T07

• Adjusts the amount of blowby gas conducted to the intake air system according to the intake manifold vacuum.

• Regulates the air (including blowby gas) passing from the cylinder head cover to intake manifold during low load (when vacuum in the intake manifold is high) to ensure an optimum air/fuel ratio.
