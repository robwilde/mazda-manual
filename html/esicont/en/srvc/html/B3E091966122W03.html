WIPER AND WASHER SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091966122W03

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Front scuff plate (Driver's side) (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Front side trim (Driver's side) (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Lower panel (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Column cover (See COLUMN COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Disconnect the wiper and washer switch connector.

4. Remove the screws, then remove the wiper and washer switch.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
