DRIVELINE/AXLE NEW FEATURES

B3E030001018T01

Improved driveability
• Unit-design, double angular ball bearings with low rotational resistance adopted for front and rear axles
• Bell-shaped constant velocity joint adopted for wheel-side joint of front drive shaft
• Tripod-shaped constant velocity joint adopted for differential-side joint of front drive shaft
Reduced vibration and noise
• Bell-shaped constant velocity joint adopted for wheel-side joint of front drive shaft
• Tripod-shaped constant velocity joint adopted for differential-side joint of front drive shaft (ATX and MTX (LH))
• Double offset-shaped constant velocity joint adopted for differential-side joint of front drive shaft (MTX (RH))
Improved serviceability
• Unit bearings that require no preload adjustment adopted for the front and rear wheels
