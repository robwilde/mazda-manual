PASSENGER AIR BAG DEACTIVATION (PAD) SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E081066123W04

1. Detach the bonnet release lever from the lower panel. (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L.H.D.].) (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [R.H.D.].)

2. Remove the front scuff plate. (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the front side trim. (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the lower panel. (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5. Remove the PAD switch.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.

7. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position.

8. Verify that the air bag system warning light illuminates for approx. 6 s and goes out.

• If the air bag system warning light does not operate normally, refer to the on-board diagnostic system (air bag system) and perform inspection of the system.
