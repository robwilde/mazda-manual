DTC C1470, C1994 [DSC]

B3E040243000W25

DTC

C1470, C1994

TCS/DSC control system

DETECTION CONDITION

• C1470
- Due to excessive braking, TCS control using braking is temporarily inhibited.
• C1994
- DSC control continues for a specified period of time or more

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• This does not indicate a malfunction since constant TCS or DSC control over an extended period of time is inhibited to protect the DSC solenoid valve inside the DSC HU or the engine.
