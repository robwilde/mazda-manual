FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER DISPOSAL

B3E021334700W02

1. Place the shock absorber on a level surface or with the piston pointing downwards.

2. Drill a 2-3 mm {0.08-0.11 in} hole at the point A shown in the figure so that the gas can escape.

Warning
• Whenever drilling into a shock absorber, wear protective eye wear. The gas in the shock absorber is pressurized and could spray metal chips into the eyes and face.

3. Drill a 2-3 mm {0.08-0.11 in} hole at the point B shown in the figure to drain the oil.

4. Turn the hole made in Step 3 downwards and drain the oil by pumping the piston rod up and down several times.

5. Cut off the end of the shock absorber.

6. Dispose of waste oil according to local waste disposal laws.
