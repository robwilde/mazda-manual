INSTRUMENT CLUSTER OUTLINE

B3E092255430T01

•  The CAN system has been adopted for the control signals of the input/output communication circuit of the meters, gauges and warning and indicator lights. (See CONTROLLER AREA NETWORK (CAN) SYSTEM OUTLINE.)

• LEDs have been adopted for warning and indicator lights installed on the instrument cluster.

• The information display, which includes the clock, audio system, and A/C system displays, has been placed in the center of the instrument panel. It also includes the drive information system, depending on the vehicle grade.

• A trumpet-type horn with spiral, resonant pipes, has been adopted.
