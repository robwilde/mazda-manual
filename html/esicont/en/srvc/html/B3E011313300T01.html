AIR CLEANER CONSTRUCTION [ZJ, Z6]

• The air cleaner is mainly composed of the air cleaner case, air cleaner cover and air cleaner element.

Air Cleaner Element

• Non-woven fabric (dry type) has been adopted.

Air Cleaner Construction

• The air cleaner construction suppresses intake air resistance for increased torque at all engine speeds.

Air Cleaner Cover

• The PCM and attached PCM case have been integrated with the cover. The PCM is forced cooled by intake air due to this integration. (See: CONTROL SYSTEM, PCM Control.)
