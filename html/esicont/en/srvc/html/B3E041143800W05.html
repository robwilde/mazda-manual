POWER BRAKE UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION (R.H.D.)

B3E041143800W05

1. Perform the following procedure and move the cooler hose (LO) to the position shown in the figure. (ZJ, Z6)

(1) Remove the suction hose.
(2) Detach the accelerator cable and move it out of the way.

(3) Remove the coolant reserve tank from the vehicle and move it out of the way.
(4) Remove the cooler hose (LO) installation nuts.

2. Perform the following procedure and move the cooler hose (LO) under the vehicle. (LF)

(1) Disconnect the HO2S connector.
(2) Remove the bracket.
(3) Remove the cooler hose bracket.

3. Remove the master cylinder. (See MASTER CYLINDER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the brake switch connector.

5. Remove the accelerator pedal. (See BRAKE PEDAL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

6. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

7. Install in the reverse order of removal.

8. After installation, inspect the brake pedal. (See BRAKE PEDAL INSPECTION.)

9. Adjust the accelerator cable. (See ACCELERATOR CABLE INSPECTION/ADJUSTMENT [ZJ, Z6].) (See ACCELERATOR CABLE INSPECTION/ADJUSTMENT [LF].)
