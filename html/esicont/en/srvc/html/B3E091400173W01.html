THEFT-DETERRENT SIREN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [5HB]

B3E091400173W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Rear seat (See REAR SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Trunk end trim (See TRUNK END TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(5) Trunk side upper trim (See TRUNK SIDE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6) Cargo compartment light (See CARGO COMPARTMENT LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Bolt
2
Theft-deterrent siren
3
Connector

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
