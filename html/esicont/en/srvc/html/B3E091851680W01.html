FRONT FOG LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851680W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the under cover.

3. Slightly bend back the mudguard.

4. Disconnect the front fog light connector.

5. Remove the screws, then remove the front fog light.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.

7. Adjust the front fog light aiming. (See FRONT FOG LIGHT AIMING.)
