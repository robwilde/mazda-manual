REAR MANUAL WINDOW REGULATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091272561W01

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Beltline molding (See REAR BELTLINE MOLDING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Sail garnish (5HB) (See SAIL GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Rear door garnish (5HB) (See REAR DOOR GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Regulator handle (See REGULATOR HANDLE REMOVAL.)
(5) Rear door trim (See REAR DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6) Rear door glass guide and glass run channel
(7) Rear door glass (See REAR DOOR GLASS REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Remove the nuts and the bolts.

3. Remove the rear manual window regulator through the rear door unit hole.

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
