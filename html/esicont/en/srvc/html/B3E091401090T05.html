LATCH AND LOCK ACTUATOR CONSTRUCTION

B3E091401090T05

• The following parts are built into the latch and lock actuators of each door and liftgate:

- A latch switch that turns on/off with the opening/closing of the door or liftgate.
• When the door or liftgate is open, the latch switch is on.
• When the door or liftgate is closed, the latch switch is off.
- A lock actuator that latches/unlatches the latch.

• The following parts are built into the lock and latch actuator of the driver-side door:

- Door key cylinder switch
- Door lock-link switch
