C-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091768250W01

4SD

1. Remove the rear seat. (See REAR SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Remove the rear scuff plate. (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the tire house trim. (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Partially peel back the seaming welt.

5. Remove the fastener.

6. Detach clips A, locator pin B and C, using a fastener remover.

7. Detach the tab from the body and remove the C-pillar trim.

8. Install in the reverse order of removal.

5HB

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Rear seat (See REAR SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Trunk side upper trim (See TRUNK SIDE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Partially peel back the seaming welt.

3. Remove the fasteners.

4. Detach clips A, B and locator pins C, using a fastener remover.

5. Remove the C-pillar trim.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
