GLOVE COMPARTMENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091764260W01

1. Remove the front scuff plate. (Passenger's side) (SeeFRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Remove the front side trim. (Passenger's side) (SeeFRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove the decoration panel. (SeeDECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the screws.

5. Pull the glove compartment outward and detach clips A and tab B.

6. Remove the glove compartment.

7. Install in the reverse order of removal.
