TRUNK SIDE UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E0917688T0W02

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (RH only)

2. Remove the cover from the trunk side upper trim.

3. Remove the cap.

4. Remove the screw.

5. Pull the trunk side upper trim outward, then disengage clips A from the body.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
