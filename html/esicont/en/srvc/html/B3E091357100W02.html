Warning
• Handling the front seat (with a side air bag and a pre-tensioner seat belt) improperly can accidentally deploy the side air bag and pre-tensioner seat belt, which may seriously injure you. Read the service warnings before handling the front seat (with a side air bag and a pre-tensioner seat belt). (See SERVICE WARNINGS.)
