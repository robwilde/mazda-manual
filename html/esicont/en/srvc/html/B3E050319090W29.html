B3E050319090W29

25

Engine stalls when driving at slow speeds or stopping

DESCRIPTION

• Engine stalls when the brake pedal is depressed while driving at low speed or stopping.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Malfunction is in engine control system (e.g. Fuel injection control, IAC system). Otherwise, the malfunction is in the control valve body, shift solenoid E or TCC.
