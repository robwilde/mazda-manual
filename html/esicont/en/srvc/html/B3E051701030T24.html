FEEDBACK CONTROL OPERATION

B3E051701030T24

• When shifting, real-time feedback correction of the clutch engagement pressure is operated by the duty-cycle solenoid valves so that the speed change of the turbine shaft (change of the turbine rotating speed) matches the predetermined target value.

• Also, the clutch engagement pressure is optimally corrected so as to absorb the changes in engine performance and/or of elapsed transaxle, according to the shift results in the past.
