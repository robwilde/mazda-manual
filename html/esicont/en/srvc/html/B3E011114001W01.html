ENGINE OIL LEVEL INSPECTION [ZJ, Z6]

B3E011114001W01

1. Position the vehicle on level ground.

2. Warm up the engine.

3. Stop the engine and allow approx. 5 min before continuing.

4. Remove the dipstick and verify that the oil level is between the F and L marks on the dipstick.

• If the oil level is below the L mark, add engine oil.
