FRONT SHOCK ABSORBER CONSTRUCTION

B3E021334700T01

• Excellent response shock absorbers with large-diameter (35 mm {1.4 in}) pistons have been adopted.

• Separated input type front shock absorber mounts have been adopted. Due to this, the shock absorbers characteristic function efficiently, improving handling and riding comfort.

• The coil springs are positioned to provide reduced friction when lateral force acts on the front shock absorbers.
