TRUNK LID ADJUSTMENT

B3E091056801W02

1. Measure the gap and height difference between the trunk lid and the body.

2. Loosen the trunk lid hinge installation bolts and adjust the trunk lid.

• If necessary, loosen the trunk lid lock striker installation bolts and adjust the trunk lid. (See TRUNK LID LOCK STRIKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

Standard clearance
a: 4.0-8.0 mm {0.16-0.31 in}
b: 2.5-4.5 mm {0.10-0.17 in}
c: -0.5-1.5 mm {-0.019-0.059 in}
d: 3.3-5.3 mm {0.13-0.20 in}

3. Tighten the bolts.
