METER HOOD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091758800W01

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Front scuff plate (Driver's side) (SeeFRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Front side trim (Driver's side) (SeeFRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Decoration panel (SeeDECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Lower panel (SeeLOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Detach the fit of the meter hood rubber from the upper column cover.

3. Pull the meter hood outward and detach the tabs A and locator pins B.

4. Remove the meter hood.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
