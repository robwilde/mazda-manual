CIGARETTE LIGHTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E092067000W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Remove the ashtray panel. (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

3. Remove in the order indicated in the table.

1
Cigarette lighter plug
2
Socket (See Socket Removal Note.)
3
Ring
(See Ring Removal Note.)

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Socket Removal Note

1. Insert a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver into the socket hole and push on the ring projection to lift the socket outward.

2. Lift out the socket until the ring projection locks into the lower socket hole as shown in the figure.

3. From the rear of the ashtray, insert a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver between the socket and the ring as shown in the figure.

4. While lifting the ring projection with the flathead screwdriver, push out and remove the socket.

Ring Removal Note

1. Pull the ring outward while pressing the tabs.
