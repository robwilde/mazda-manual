ENGINE DISASSEMBLY/ASSEMBLY [LF]

B3E011001001W05

1. Disconnect the engine and transaxle. (See AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].) (See MANUAL TRANSAXLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [F35M-R].)

2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Intake-air system (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].)
(2) Generator (See GENERATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].)
(3) Ignition coils (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].)
(4) Crankshaft position (CKP) sensor (See CRANKSHAFT POSITION (CKP) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [LF].)

3. Assemble in the reverse order of disassembly.
