AUTO LEVELING CONTROL MODULE CONSTRUCTION/OPERATION

B3E000001052T22

• The auto leveling control module is located inside of the passenger's side dashboard.

• The auto leveling control module verifies changes in vehicle speed and attitude based on signal inputted from the PJB and the auto leveling sensor. The control module then calculates the optimal direction for the optical axis.

• Based on the calculation of the optical axis adjustment amount, the auto leveling control module controls the headlight leveling actuator.
