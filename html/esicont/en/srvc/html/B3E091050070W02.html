REAR BUMPER REINFORCEMENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091050070W02

1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Trunk end trim (See TRUNK END TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(2) Trunk side trim (4SD) (See TRUNK SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(3) Trunk side trim (RH) (5HB) (See TRUNK SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(4) Service hole cover (LH) (5HB)
(5) Rear combination lights (See REAR COMBINATION LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
(6) Rear bumper (See REAR BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2. Remove the nuts, then remove the rear bumper reinforcement.

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
