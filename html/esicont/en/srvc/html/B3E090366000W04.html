NO.3 DISPLAY SCREEN CHANGES TO WHITE.

B3E090366000W04

3

Display screen changes to white.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Car-navigation unit malfunction
• LCD unit malfunction
• Improper adjustment of the screen contrast

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

1
• Turn the ignition switch to the ACC position.
• Does the display screen change to "display quality adjustment" mode when pressing the VOICE button for approx. 2 s or more?
Yes
Go to Step 3.
No
Go to the next step.
2
• Does the display screen become white in NAVI mode?
Yes
Replace the LCD unit. (See LCD UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
No
Go to the next step.
3
• Does the contrast setting for the NAVI mode adjust to near the center?
Yes
Replace the car-navigation unit. (See CAR-NAVIGATION UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
No
Adjust the contrast of the NAVI mode near the center.
