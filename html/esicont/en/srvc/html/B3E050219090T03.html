Malfunction Detection Function

• In the malfunction detection function, the PCM detects malfunctions in the automatic transmission while driving.

• When vehicle driving conditions correspond with a preset malfunction detection condition, the PCM determines that the automatic transmission has a malfunction and stores the corresponding DTC.

• When a malfunction is detected, stored DTCs can be retrieved using the WDS or equivalent connected to the DLC-2.
