POWER STEERING OUTLINE

B3E061401034T01

• Engine-speed-sensing power steering has been adopted for vehicles with the ZJ, Z6 engine.

• For vehicles with the LF engine, excellent steering feel and smooth handling from low to high speeds is provided due to the adoption of Electro Hydraulic Power Assist Steering (EHPAS). Engine load is reduced, improving fuel economy, due to the adoption of an electric power steering oil pump.
