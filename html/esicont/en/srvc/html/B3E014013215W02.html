MASS AIR FLOW (MAF)/INTAKE AIR TEMPERATURE (IAT) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, Z6]

B3E014013215W02

1. Remove the battery cover. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, Z6].)

2. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3. Perform the following procedure for easier access

(1) Remove the battery box. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [ZJ, Z6].)

4. Remove the MAF/IAT sensor.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.

MAF/IAT sensor tightening torque
0.55-0.82 N·m {5.7-8.3 kgf·cm, 4.9-7.2 in·lbf}
