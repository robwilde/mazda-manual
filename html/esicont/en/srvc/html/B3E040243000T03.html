ON-BOARD DIAGNOSTIC SYSTEM PID/DATA MONITOR FUNCTION (ABS, DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL)

B3E040243000T03

• The PID/data monitor function is used for optionally selecting input/output signal monitor items preset in the ABS HU/CM or DSC HU/CM and reading them out in real-time.
