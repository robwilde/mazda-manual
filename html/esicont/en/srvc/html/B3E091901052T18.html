HEADLIGHT CLEANER ACTUATOR OPERATION

B3E091901052T18

• The headlight cleaner nozzle is held retracted by a spring within the headlight cleaner actuator.

• When fluid pressure rises due to the operation of the headlight cleaner motor, the piston in the headlight cleaner actuator is pushed, causing the nozzle to pop out of the front bumper and spray washer fluid.
