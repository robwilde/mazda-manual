SUNROOF MOTOR INSPECTION

B3E091569873W02

1. Measure the voltage at each terminal (other than terminal G).

• If not as specified, inspect the parts listed under "Inspection item" and the related wiring harnesses.

2. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

3. Verify that continuity at terminal G is as indicated in the Terminal Voltage Table (Reference).

4. If the parts and wiring harnesses are normal but the system still does not work properly, replace the sunroof relay.
