RADIATOR GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091650710W01

4SD

1. Remove the screws.

2. Press clip A in the direction of the arrow, pull the radiator grille outward and remove from the front bumper.

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.

5HB

1. Remove the nuts.

2. Detach clips A and remove the radiator grille.

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
