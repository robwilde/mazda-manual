FRONT SIDE TURN LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

B3E091851121W01

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

2. Move the front side turn light in the direction of the arrow (1) and detach the tab.

3. Pull area A of the front side turn light in the direction of the arrow (2) and remove the front side turn light.

4. Disconnect the front side turn light connector.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
