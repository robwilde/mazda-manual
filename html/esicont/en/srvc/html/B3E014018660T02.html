CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION (CPP) SWITCH CONSTRUCTION/OPERATION [ZJ, Z6]

B3E014018660T02

• When the clutch pedal is depressed, the contact closes (ON) and the PCM detects a voltage of 1.0 V or less. When the clutch pedal is not depressed, the contact opens (OFF) the PCM detects a voltage of B+.
