NO.21 NOISE OCCURS AT IDLE WHEN VEHICLE IS STOPPED IN D, M RANGES, OR IN R POSITION

B3E050319090W25

21

Noise occurs at idle when vehicle is stopped in D, M ranges, or in R position

DESCRIPTION

• Transaxle is noisy in D, M ranges, or in R position when vehicle is idling.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

• Although the malfunction is basically the same as No.20 "NOISE OCCURS AT IDLE WHEN VEHICLE IS STOPPED IN ALL POSITIONS/RANGES", other causes may include selector lever position disparity or TR switch position disparity.
